Sites to examine

The unsourced extension only wants to examine news articles. It has a built-in list of news sites, and will also check web pages for hnews, schema.org or opengraph data.

You can also add your own list of sites to treat as news sites.

  • One site per line
  • leading dot matches any subdomain eg ".example.com" matches "www.example.com" and "bad.example.com"
  • use trailing "..." as wildcard eg "example.com/news/..."
